SEMANTICA is a design system built for teams who care about craft.

## What is SEMANTICA?

SEMANTICA gives your team a shared language — from colour and typography to spacing and motion.

## Key principles

- **Consistency** — every component follows the same rules
- **Accessibility** — WCAG 2.1 AA compliance out of the box
- **Performance** — lightweight tokens, no runtime overhead
